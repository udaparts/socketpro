SocketPro is a world-leading secured asynchronous communication framework, a complete SocketPro software development kit with many tutorial and sample projects for C++, Java, .NET, Python and Node.js as well as JavaScript for across-platform and cros-language developments.

SocketPro is also next generation technology that streams both requests and responses. It is written from scratch with continuous in-line request/result batching, real-time stream sending/processing, asynchronous data transferring and parallel computation in mind. It offers superior performance and scalability with many unique and critical features. These features will definitely solve your daunting issues and meet your complex needs. Consequently, SocketPro will help you quickly develop high-quality distributed applications on major platforms such as Windows, Linux variants and browsers as well as Apple and devices in the future.
